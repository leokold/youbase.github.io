Contextual Thinking

Cortex is an application for contextual thought and collaboration. Powered by YouBase's Distributed Data Protocol.

It's the ultimate tool for building and sharing on an authentic internet. On the surface, it's a note-taking app, but new contexts give new powers.

A New Kind of Linking

Keylinks allow for public and private links. Each note has cryptographic keys identified by your personal domain and path.

Alice.me/Blog or another path you choose and the content that lives there becomes yours and yours only in the world. In essence, human-readable key names work like file paths or URLs. You can even use your current crypto current keys as the beginning of new note structures, giving your current keys a whole new power. Keylinks that aren’t for your indentity don’t open. Markup makes it all flexible.

NFTs in Context

As each note has keys for a valid crypto address. Each note can be validated by the note owner, can be used as an ID and can be used to carry NFTs. Digital assets in a whole new context.

Ownership on the Go

While you can prove ownership, nobody else can prove what you own without you OKing that approval. Organize, carry and access all your digital stuff wherever you go.

Smart Identity

Show a barcode to prove anything you own is yours and current. Others can also digitally sign for additional verification of aspects of your authentic self.

Data Access and Storage Control

You decide who has access to your notes. You hold the keys. Neither Cortex nor anyone else can access your notes without your permission. Everything’s encrypted end to end, unless you make it public.

Your encrypted data will be able to live where you choose, locally, IPFS, AWS or just about anywhere.

Channels for Sharing and Collaboration

Keep things private for yourself, between you and one other, a group, or publish to the web to collaborate. Git-like features will manage versions, history and even forking of your creations. The history of changes will move with your creations. Simple, decentralized and provable.

Earn Recognition and Coin

With keys, of course, there’s also a financial context. You can quickly turn anything you create into a digital asset and receive coin where your content lives.

Individual notes or entire trees of notes can become shareable, tradeable NFTs.

Topics and Social Search

Notes have topics and links that allow you to share, search and contribute across your social sphere and the metaverse. Then search in a social context see what the people you care about are saying on a topic.

Globally Unique

Butterfly Protocol domain names will act as global IDs on Cortex. Cortex-specific domains (NFT-tree based) will be available with Cortex utility tokens.

With these names on Cortex, you'll make a great impression and be able to open up some unique features and earn based on your contributions.

The domain will serve as an ID but also an address for your creations and posts.

Open Source

The Technology under the hood in the Cortex application is the YouBase Distributed Data Protocol (YDDP). YDDP will be open source.

YDDP connects data to HD crypto wallets in some unique and easy to use ways while separating data structure from data location.
